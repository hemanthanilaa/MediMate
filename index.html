Track Prescriptions. Understand Your Treatment. Stay on Schedule.

MediMate helps you manage prescriptions, view history, and get summaries of your treatment all in one secure place.

Get Started

Key Features

Prescription Upload

Upload images or PDFs. AI reads and extracts the text using OCR.

Prescription Summary

Get a clean, easy-to-read summary of medications and instructions.

History Tracking

View previous prescriptions by date. Track changes and progress.

Reminders

Configure SMS or email reminders to never miss a dose.

How It Works

  1. Sign up securely using your email.
  2. Upload prescriptions using phone or laptop.
  3. Let AI summarize and organize your prescriptions.
  4. View medical history and get medication alerts.
